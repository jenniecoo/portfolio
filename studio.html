Big Data Platforms

For business in the industrial internet world, building apps to support thier complex business needs is familar, but building cloud based and on premise environments is another set of skills altogether. Platform-As-A-Service solutions provide an infrastructure to develope and launch apps, so that our customers can focus on thier customers needs first.

However, designing for an Industrial Internet platform is very different from designing for an Industrial Internet product. With the developers as your users, the focus shifts to providing an tool suite for accelerated developement, shared services, and economies of scale.

Below is an overview of some key projects I have worked on in this discipline. The documentation and results of these projects are highly confidential and thus limited on this site; please contact me if you would like to learn more about my work.

Studio on Predix

Predix Studio is low code app development concept that empowers users to create applications which are bespoke to thier business needs. From first principles I developed a overarching story for the app creation tool, through visualization and data connection to publishing and deployment. Following the acquisition of a company with an aligned product story, my role pivoted to assessing and testing the usability of the acquired app, and providing a vision for a future merged concept, bringing the current product inline with the future vision.

Product Definition and Software Lifecycle

Recently I have been supporting the internal business to help define a new product offering, which has involved clarifying and validating the problem with potential customer, developing a concept journey map for the future customer experience, and designing communication pieces to help circulate the mission both internally and externally. Information on this project is limited as it has not been released externally.
